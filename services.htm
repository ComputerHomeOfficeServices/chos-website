Overview of Services

Computer Home and Office Services specializes in the setup, troubleshooting, and installation of all things related to Microsoft Windows-based personal computers. We specialize in computers as typically used in homes and small businesses.

If you are thinking about upgrading your computer with new memory, need a modem replaced, or have another internal part replaced, Computer Home and Office Services can install it. We also install software and operating systems.

For those wanting to add Internet service or start a home network, we can also help here with the installation of network cards, hubs, routers, and wireless network equipment. If you have an existing wireless network, we can secure it so that your neighbors don't take away your bandwidth.

If your computer is not working, not working well enough, or needs to be optimized for better performance, troubleshooting services are available. In order to help prevent a problem from really setting you back, we also offer a backup service. Another service is the removal of viruses and spyware.

Also available are tutoring services for general computer use, how to use the Internet, and some select software programs. We are experts in the use of Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, etc.) and similar office software packages. Custom template documents will be created on request.
